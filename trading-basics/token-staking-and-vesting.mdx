`$LOGX` is the native governance token of the LogX Network, serving multiple purposes within the ecosystem. By staking $LOGX, users can participate in governance, earn rewards, and benefit from various features that the LogX Network offers.

## **Key Features of $LOGX Staking**

### **Governance Rights**

$LOGX holders will have governance rights within the LogX ecosystem.

### **Gas Token**

`$LOGX` will serve as the gas token for the LogX Network. `$LOGX` will be required to facilitate transactions and interactions within the ecosystem.

### **Trading Discounts and Rewards**

`$LOGX` holders will enjoy additional trading fee discounts and higher trading rewards.

### **Staking rewards**

Stakers of `$LOGX` receive `$LOGX` as Staking Rewards. Trading fees and rewards may also be used for campaigns including trading competitions, buyback, token burn and airdrop.

## **How to Deposit $LOGX**

To stake $LOGX and start earning rewards, follow these steps:

1. **Deposit $LOGX tokens** on the LogX Network through LogX's Native Bridge Feature.
2. Once deposited, `$LOGX` can be staked within the LogX staking platform.
3. Stake $LOGX and start earning rewards.

### **Earning $LOGX Airdrops**

In addition to staking, $LOGX airdrops are available for users who actively trade on LOGX Network. Rewards get credited in realtime.

## **Staking Rewards**

The tokens that we buyback with fees are distributed to stakers as rewards. 

You can unstake your $LOGX anytime with unstaking period of 1 hour.
